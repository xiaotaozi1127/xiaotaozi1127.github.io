Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This app may be used by or with children. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable data protection laws.

1. Who We Are

StoryTeller is developed and operated by xiaotaozi1127 ("we", "us", or "our"). If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at xiaotaozi1127@hotmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the StoryTeller service:

  • Account information: When you register, we collect your name and email address to create and manage your account.
  • User-generated content: Stories, text, and other creative content you create or save within the app.
  • Device information: Basic technical information such as device type and operating system version, used solely for app functionality and bug fixes.

We do not collect payment information, precise location data, or sensitive personal information.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

StoryTeller is designed for use by families. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent.

  • If a parent or guardian creates an account on behalf of a child, the parent's or guardian's contact information is used.
  • Children's user-generated content (stories) is stored securely and not shared publicly without explicit parental consent.
  • If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13 without parental consent, please contact us immediately at xiaotaozi1127@hotmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the StoryTeller app.
  • Manage your account and authenticate your identity.
  • Save and sync your stories and content across devices.
  • Respond to your support requests and communications.
  • Send important service-related notifications (e.g., security alerts).

We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we do not use your data for targeted advertising.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating the app (e.g., cloud storage, analytics), bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may transfer to the new owner, who will be bound by this Privacy Policy.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and protected using industry-standard encryption and security practices. We regularly review our security procedures to guard against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information.

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information and user-generated content for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at xiaotaozi1127@hotmail.com.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at xiaotaozi1127@hotmail.com.
